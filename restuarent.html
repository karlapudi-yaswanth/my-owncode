SERVED EVERY DAY SINCE 1985

Bubby’s opened on Thanksgiving Day 1985. Owner Ram krishna began baking pies and selling them to restaurants and his neighbors out of a small kitchen at the corner of ongole.
Today, NYC’s beloved restaurant and pie shop celebrates 27 years of classic, made from scratch village cooking.


Why It's Effective :

than calling itself a “New York institution” or using the word "reliable," Bubby’s uses its headline to make a specific claim — ”Served every day since 1985” — that demonstrates those ideas. This captures more attention and paints a richer, more vivid picture of its history and role in the community.
The rest of the description supports this by adding details to the story of its founding and explaining that even three decades later, its scratch approach to cooking remains the same.
